Koraput, in southern Odisha, is a scenic, tribal-rich district known for its hilly terrain, waterfalls, and coffee plantations. Deomali, located about 35-60 km from Koraput town near Doodhari village, is the highest peak in Odisha (1,672m). It offers breathtaking views, cool breezes, and is a popular destination for trekking, nature, and photography in the Eastern Ghats.
