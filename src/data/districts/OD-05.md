Historic Baliyatra Cuttack, acclaimed as Asia’s largest open-air trade fair, was held from November 5–13, 2025, on 60 acres along the Mahanadi River. Celebrating Odisha’s maritime legacy, the event attracted over 70 lakh visitors, 2,500+ stalls, and generated ₹4,000 crore in trade, featuring cultural shows and top artist like Shreya Ghosal or Sonu Nigan.
